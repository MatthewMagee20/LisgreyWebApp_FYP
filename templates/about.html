About Us

Our Family Run Steakhouse Bar, Lounge and restaurant is a comfortable setting to a enjoy a relaxing and satisfying meal with friends and family or that special someone.

A favourite among locals, we provide a wide selection of food for all occasions. We pride ourselves on our house steaks especially the customer favourite, the famous ‘Steak on the stone’.

Our menu caters for every type of taste bud including vegetarians and small children also.

We hold small functions such as communions, confirmations, birthday parties and anniversaries. All very welcome.
